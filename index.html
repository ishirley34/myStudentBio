Ian Shirley



Ian Shirley was born in Bogota, Colombia in 1989 and lived there until he was 5 years old. At the age of 5 he moved to the Hosuton area and lived there until he went off to college at Texas A&M Univeristy at Galveston. While in College he worked at the univeristy's helpdesk, there he helped stuedents, faculty, and staff with all electronic issues ranging from cellphones and printers to computers and projectors
In college Ian studied Marine Science and worked in the dive program at TAMUG. While in the dive program he got his full technical cave certification through NSS: CDS and IANTD. In his senior year Ian was hired to work the SEG booth for SinoGEO in San Antonio. This served as a nice boost to his resume which eventually helped Ian land a job an internship at Fugro Sesimic Imaging. Ian graduated from Texas A&M with a B.Sc in Marine Science in May of 2012 and was hired full time at Fugro as an Associate Geophysicist.
